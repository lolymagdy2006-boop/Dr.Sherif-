Strategic Problem Solving

Designing a strategic response to complex problems requires deep analysis, stakeholder collaboration, and iterative thinking. Complex problems are not solved by isolated decisions, but by understanding systems, constraints, and user needs.

Co-Design Strategy

Co-design ensures solutions are realistic, scalable, and aligned with real-world expectations by involving designers, developers, and users from the beginning.
